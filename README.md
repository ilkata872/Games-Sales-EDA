The notebook answers several business questions through visualization:

Peak Release Years: Analyzed the volume of game releases per year, identifying the era with the highest output (around 2008-2009).

Top Genres: Visualized cumulative global sales by genre. Result: Action and Sports are the leading categories.

Publisher Dominance: Ranked the top 10 publishers by global sales. Result: Nintendo and Electronic Arts lead the market.

Platform Wars: Compared total software sales across different gaming consoles. Result: The PS2 stands out as the highest-selling software platform.
